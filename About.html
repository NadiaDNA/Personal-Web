Let's read My stories!

I have a background in Chemical Engineering and started my career in the FMCG industry as a

Group Leader in chocolate production. Stepping into a leadership role right after graduate was

both challenging and rewarding. That experience shaped me a lot. It taught me how to lead not

only a team, but also myself. I learned to stay adaptable, solve problems under pressure, and

grow together with the people I worked with.


Currently, I work as a freelance science teacher. Teaching feels more like a calling to me. I truly

enjoy witnessing growth, whether it's in myself or in my students. Guiding others and sharing

knowledge inspires me to keep learning too.


Now, I'm also exploring a new journey in web development. I see it as another exciting field that I

can keep learning, experimenting, and building meaningful things. beside of work, I love exploring

creativity through food, science, and technology especially sharing the journey along the way.
